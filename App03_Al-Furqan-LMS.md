# Privacy Policy for Al-Furqan LMS Mobile Application

**Last Updated:** July 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction & Overview
Welcome to **Al-Furqan LMS** (the "Application"), a mobile application built upon the open-source Moodle™ framework designed to provide an optimized, accessible, and seamless mobile learning experience. 

We take user privacy exceptionally seriously. This Privacy Policy clarifies how data interacts with the application, what information is processed, and our strict commitment to safeguarding your digital environment.

---

## 2. No Personal Data Collection or Processing
**NO PERSONAL DATA IS COLLECTED, STORED, OR PROCESSED DIRECTLY THROUGH THE AL-FURQAN LMS MOBILE APPLICATION.**

The Application operates strictly as a secure client portal/interface wrapper to connect you to your designated educational institution's learning servers. We have no interest in tracking, harvesting, profiling, or gathering data beyond the pure technical functionality required to render your educational workspace on your device.

---

## 3. Architecture & Data Handling
Because the Application connects to an external learning management instance, it is vital to understand where your data resides:

* **Direct Client-to-Server Connection:** When you log in, input assignments, or view course content within the Application, all transactional data and user credentials travel directly between your mobile device and the backend servers hosted by your educational organization or service provider.
* **No Middleware Interception:** We do not route your traffic through intermediate analytical servers, nor do we store logs of your personal activities, names, enrollment metrics, grades, or locations. 
* **Local Caching:** To ensure an efficient and responsive interface, certain static assets (such as application layout structures and performance-optimized font files) may be temporarily cached locally on your device. No user-identifiable records are extracted during this optimization routine.

---

## 4. Third-Party Integrations & External Hosting
While the Al-Furqan LMS application itself does not collect personal information, the external educational installations, servers, or plugins managed by your institution may capture data necessary for grading, attendance, and course delivery. 

* **Institutional Compliance:** The collection, retention, and handling of student or user data on those connected installations are governed exclusively by your educational institution's independent privacy policy and compliance frameworks.
* **External Links:** If you navigate to external web resources or third-party plugins within your courses, data processing policies will be dictated by those respective third-party suppliers. We encourage you to review their specific documentation.

---

## 5. Regulatory Compliance & Global Standards
To ensure the absolute highest level of user protection, our framework structures reflect global privacy standards, including the European Union’s **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and relevant regional data compliance mandates. 

Since the application does not pool, aggregate, or retain personal records on external databases, your "Right to be Forgotten," right to access, and right to rectification remain completely intact and manageable directly through your primary educational institution's data controller.

---

## 6. Updates to This Privacy Policy
We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to align with new application builds, framework enhancements, or evolving global data standards. Any modifications will be reflected immediately by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

---

## 7. Contact Information
If you have questions regarding the application's configuration, performance optimization routines, or technical data routing architecture, please reach out via your institution's system administrator or technical support channel.
